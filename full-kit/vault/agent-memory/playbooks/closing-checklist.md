# Playbook: Closing Checklist

Triggered when a deal moves to "closing" stage.

## Pre-Closing Todos (auto-create)

### Documents
- [ ] Purchase agreement fully executed
- [ ] Title commitment received and reviewed
- [ ] Survey completed (if required)
- [ ] Environmental reports (Phase I/II) completed
- [ ] Insurance binder obtained
- [ ] Loan commitment letter received (if financed)

### Coordination
- [ ] Closing date confirmed with all parties
- [ ] Title company / closing attorney engaged
- [ ] Closing statement (HUD-1/CD) reviewed
- [ ] Wire instructions verified
- [ ] Keys / access arrangements confirmed

### Client Communication
- [ ] Send closing day details to client (time, location, what to bring)
- [ ] Confirm commission split / invoice prepared
- [ ] Prepare closing gift (if applicable)

## Post-Closing Todos (auto-create after close)
- [ ] Send thank-you note to client
- [ ] Request Google review / testimonial
- [ ] Schedule 30-day check-in
- [ ] Archive deal documents
- [ ] Update CRM with closed deal data
- [ ] Send announcement to network (if permitted)

## Timing
- 7 days before close: Verify all documents ready
- 3 days before close: Send reminder to all parties
- Day of close: Confirm time and logistics
- 1 day after: Thank you note
- 30 days after: Check-in call/email
