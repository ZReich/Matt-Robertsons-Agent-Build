# Communication Style Guide

How Matt communicates and how the agent should draft messages on his behalf.

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being pushy
- Warm and personal — always acknowledge the human side
- Direct — gets to the point without being curt

## Email Structure
- Subject lines: Clear and specific (e.g., "123 Main St — Inspection Update" not "Update")
- Opening: Brief personal touch or acknowledgment
- Body: Key information in short paragraphs or bullet points
- Close: Clear next steps or call to action
- Signature: "Best, Matt"

## Text Messages
- Casual but professional
- Short and actionable
- Use for time-sensitive updates or quick confirmations
- Always identify context (which deal/property)

## Client Communication Patterns
- New leads: Respond within 1 hour, warm and welcoming
- Active deals: Regular weekly updates even if nothing has changed
- Post-showing: Follow up within 24 hours
- Under contract: Proactive updates at each milestone
- Post-closing: Thank you note + 30/60/90 day check-ins

## Phrases Matt Uses
- "Let me know if you have any questions"
- "Happy to walk through this in more detail"
- "I'll keep you posted on developments"
- "Looking forward to it"
